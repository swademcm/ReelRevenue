Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Go to Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately for upgrades, or at the next billing cycle for downgrades.

How does the AI content generator work?

Our AI analyzes successful content in your niche and generates original posts, scripts, and captions optimized for engagement and conversions. You can customize tone, style, and target audience.

Is there a limit on content generation?

Limits vary by plan. Creator plan includes 100 AI generations per month, Publisher includes 500, and Maximizer includes unlimited generations.

What affiliate networks do you support?

We integrate with major affiliate networks including Amazon Associates, ClickBank, ShareASale, CJ Affiliate, and many others. We're constantly adding new integrations.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
